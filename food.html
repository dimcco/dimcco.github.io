DIMCCO - Turkey's Finest Extra Olive Oil - EVOO


It all started with our desire to make the best Turkish Extra Virgin Olive Oil. Our family was born and raised in the fertile lands. Therefore, we are able to take advantage of knowledge and possibilities of unique characteristics of Saurani olive trees. As a family brand company, Dimcco LLC rents the land locally in the area where our olive groves are located in the inner Aegean region.


Saurani olives are known throughout the world for making the finest Extra Virgin Olive Oil. Our brand, Dimcco LLC, offers the widest selection of fine Turkish products all in one place.


The climate conditions where our groves are located and the fruity roots aroma creates a unique olive oil flavor. Our olives are early harvested by hand on ladders at the ideal moment to have very low acidity and cold pressed immediately to pruduce a superior olive oil.


All of our oils are produced at Koprubasi, Manisa ranch where quality is paramount. Extra Virgin Olive Oil is essentially freshly pressed fruit juice. And so we crush the olives and extract oil using only mechanical methods, never using any high heat or solvents.


Our Food Safety Management System is provided by an independent company which ensures food safety and quality throughout our processing,storage,sales and delivery to our customer. This independent service system meets national and international standards in compliance with ISO 9001, ISO 2200, USDA ORGANIC, U.S. FDA Registered and GFSI / FSSC 22000 International Food Safety Management standard documents and is regularly audited by the institutions that issue these certifications.


Food Safety and Quality


Dimcco olive oils are packeged in glass or tin containers which maintain our high standard for Hygienic standards from processing to shipping.



Advantages of Glass and Tin Containers:


  • Preserves the quality of characteristic taste and aroma of olive oils its shelf life.
  • Does not let the moisture and odor from the outdoor environment to penetrate olive oil.
  • Hygienic,recyclable and environmentally friendly.


Our Workforce and Quality Control


Our company employs, trains and supervises local workers to grow and pick only select olives of highest quality.
